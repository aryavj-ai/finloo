# Finance Tracker - Android Installation Guide

## How to Install on Your Android Device

This is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that works like a native Android app once installed!

### Installation Steps:

#### Method 1: Using Chrome Browser (Recommended)
1. Open Chrome on your Android device
2. Navigate to the Finance Tracker website
3. Tap the **three dots menu** (â‹®) in the top right
4. Select **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"** to confirm
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

#### Method 2: Using the In-App Prompt
1. When you first visit the site, you may see an install banner at the top
2. Tap **"Install"** on the banner
3. The app will be added to your home screen

### Usage:

Once installed, simply tap the Finance Tracker icon on your home screen to open the app. It will work just like any other Android app!

### System Requirements:

- Android 5.0 or higher
- Chrome browser (recommended) or any modern browser
- No Google Play account needed
- No app store download required

### Data Privacy:

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server - your data is completely private and under your control.

### Need Help?

If the install option doesn't appear:
- Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- Try accessing the site via HTTPS
- Check that your browser supports PWAs
- Clear your browser cache and try again
