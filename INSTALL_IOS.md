This is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that works like a native iOS app once added to your home screen!

### Installation Steps for iPhone/iPad:

#### Using Safari Browser (Required for iOS)
1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone or iPad (must use Safari, not Chrome)
2. Navigate to the Finance Tracker website
3. Tap the **Share button** (â–¡ with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen

7. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

### Troubleshooting:

**Can't find "Add to Home Screen"?**
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or another browser)
- Check that you're viewing the page in normal mode (not Private Browsing)
- Update iOS to the latest version

**App not working offline?**
- iOS has limited service worker support
- Some features may require an internet connection
- Core functionality will still work without internet

**Want to delete the app?**
- Long-press the app icon on your home screen
- Tap "Remove App"
- Confirm deletion

### Removing the App:

To uninstall:
1. Long-press the Finance Tracker icon on your home screen
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Select **"Delete App"**
4. Your data will be deleted along with the app
